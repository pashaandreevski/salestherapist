The Problem

Follow-ups are broken. Win-backs are ignored. Here's why.

7x

Root cause: Follow-up tools have no emotional awareness. They track time since last message, not the quality of the last message. They know WHEN someone went silent, but not WHY. So every lead gets the same boring "just checking in" — and ignores it.

The Solution: 5 Stages of Sales Grief

An AI agent that reads emotions, remembers context, and says what every sales rep thinks but would never type.

1

DENIAL

1-2 days silent • RAG: product wins, news
Aggressively cheerful. Delusional optimism. Transparent excuses.
"COMPLETELY unrelated to you not replying — we just shipped AI routing and I physically could not stop myself from messaging you. Your team is still copy-pasting between 5 WhatsApp groups right? Sorry, rhetorical question."
2

ANGER

3-5 days silent • RAG: competitor weaknesses, pain points
Brutally direct. Uncomfortable honesty. The friend who tells you about the spinach.
"Sarah. SPREADSHEETS. In 2026. That's not a workflow, that's a cry for help. TechCorp switched to us and cut response time by 94%. But hey, spreadsheets are cool too."
3

BARGAINING

6-10 days silent • RAG: product changelog, new features
Shamelessly desperate. Holding a boombox outside your window.
"I begged my manager for 30% off. I built a custom demo and named it 'Sarah's Team.' That's either dedicated or concerning. I have nothing left to offer except my dignity. That's been gone since day 5."
4

DEPRESSION

11-20 days silent • RAG: industry case studies, testimonials
Dramatically melancholic. Sad poet who happens to know your conversion metrics.
"It's been 15 days. The team asks about you and I change the subject. I've started listening to Adele. By the way TechCorp went from 3-day response time to 4 hours. Not a guilt trip. Just information. That I'm sharing. While crying."
5

ACCEPTANCE

21+ days silent • RAG: competitive intel, FOMO triggers
Zen master with a knife. Total peace, then one devastating line.
"I've made peace. No hard feelings. Quick FYI — RetailBrand, your competitor? Signed up Tuesday. They seem happy. I'll be over here. Helping your competitor. Wishing you and your spreadsheets all the best."

Data Flow

What happens in the ~22 seconds between trigger and Telegram delivery.

1

Trigger received

Webhook fires with contact name, days silent, conversation context, and conversation ID. In production this comes from Superchat's inbound message webhook; for the demo we trigger manually.

2

AI Agent activates

Claude Sonnet 4.6 receives the input with the full SalesTherapist personality prompt. It reads the days_silent value to determine which grief stage to use.

3

RAG search (Needle Collection)

The agent searches the knowledge base for: (a) grief playbook — personality guide and stage-specific tone rules, (b) product knowledge — features, case studies, competitor data, (c) conversation context — what the lead cared about.

4

Message generation

Agent generates a 2-4 sentence WhatsApp-friendly message. Structured output: responseText + emotionalStage + reasoning. Every bold claim is grounded in RAG results — real data, not hallucination.

5

Superchat delivery

HTTP Request node POSTs to Superchat's /messages API with the contact identifier and Telegram channel ID. Message arrives in the customer's Telegram within seconds.

RAG Knowledge Base

Three markdown documents uploaded to a Needle Collection. The AI agent searches them before every message.

Hackathon MVP

What it does today

  • Manual trigger with pre-seeded conversation context
  • 3 static knowledge base documents (Needle Collection)
  • AI agent with personality prompt + RAG search
  • Single-channel delivery (Telegram via Superchat)
  • 5 grief stages based on days_silent parameter
  • Structured output: message + stage + reasoning
  • Demo trigger page with one-click stage buttons

Production Vision

Where this is going

  • Live Superchat webhook — auto-triggers on every inbound message
  • CRM connector — pulls real conversation history, not static context
  • Automatic days_silent calculation from Superchat timestamps
  • Multi-channel — WhatsApp, Instagram, Email, Telegram simultaneously
  • Tone calibration dial — adjust boldness from "professional" to "unhinged"
  • Sentiment analysis on inbound messages — detect frustration/excitement live
  • A/B testing grief stages — which stage converts best per segment
  • Analytics dashboard — track open rates, reply rates per grief stage
  • Team-wide deployment — each rep gets their own SalesTherapist tuned to their style
  • Self-learning RAG — auto-ingests new case studies, product updates, competitive intel
